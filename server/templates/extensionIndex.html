For all your journal performance needs.

The Journal of Journal Performance Studies Firefox extension provides immediate access to the latest in bibliometric and journal mapping technology. It also provides a glimpse into the future by creating special advertisements based on contemporary trends. Install the extension today!

Keep track of the journal's factors!

We all know how important it is to keep up on the latest bibliometric factors. That's why the JJPS Extension provides all of the necessary metrics from a convenient drop-down menu. We offer standard values such as SJR and the hIndex, as well as our own special ones such as the Frobpact Factor (tm), Eigenfrob Factor (tm), Frobfluence (tm), and new! The Click Value! We're always developing new ways to measure journal performance, so stay tuned!

Journal mapping

Not sure of what other journals might be owned by the same publisher? The JJPS extension provides a simple way to see all of the other journals that belong to the publisher being viewed. The more black, the more the publisher owns and the more they can influence others!

Journal prices

Need to know how much a journal costs your institution? Where possible we give you the bottom line. As well, we keep you up to date with everything going on in the world of this publisher, including headlines and real-time stock quotes!

 

And there's more!
